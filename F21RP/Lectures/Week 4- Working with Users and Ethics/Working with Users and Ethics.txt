Working with Users and Ethics

Learning Outcomes

This week the emphasis is placed on 2 topics that should concern most projects: Working with Users and Ethics. 

In a nutshell, by the end of this week, you should be able to answer the following questions:

- What are the types of data we can collect from users?
- What are the types of questions we can use in questionnaires?
- Why do I need to anonymise data?
- What are the rules for an Ethical Experiment?
- How to fill in the Ethics Form
